Companion workflow

Four deliberate beats carry an idea from prompt to action.

The flow mirrors how analysts actually work—explore, iterate, validate, deliver—while the platform takes care of orchestration. FastAPI secures access, DuckDB models the data, workers enrich context, and the LLM narrates the story.

  1. 01 Connect

    Ingest & focus

    Upload datasets or point the companion to authorised sources—files, warehouses, APIs, documented web pages. Metadata is catalogued instantly, and the assistant confirms which signals are ready before moving forward.

  2. 02 Explore

    LLM pairing

    Describe goals in natural language. Prompt guides draw on past conversations, ensuring the LLM suggests measurable hypotheses— querying datasets, enriching from APIs, or summarising authorised web context.

  3. 03 Validate

    Interrogate & justify

    Review multi-step analysis plans, generated visuals, and narrative drafts. The assistant explains each decision, proposes alternative slices, and automatically queues workers for deeper exploration or scheduled refreshes.

  4. 04 Deliver

    Package & broadcast

    Export polished decks, share live dashboards, trigger downstream alerts, or update partner tools. Decision trails and cost summaries stay linked to every hand-off.

Under the hood

Automation keeps pace with your curiosity.

FastAPI routes requests to the right services, DuckDB handles transformations in-memory, and workers take on asynchronous enrichment. Meanwhile the companion narrates impact, suggests follow-ups, and keeps context aligned for your next session.

  • Intent-aware routing

    Requests flow through services that respect roles, sensitivity, and cost ceilings before touching storage or APIs.

  • Edge analytics

    The transformation layer blends files, API payloads, and event streams at speed, powering interactive exploration without round-trips.

  • Worker choreography

    Async tasks monitor costs, refresh models, poll authorised APIs, and sync summaries downstream—so your focus stays on the storyline.
