Reliable motion

From raw files to briefing-ready intelligence in four guided moves.

Each stage blends conversational guidance, visual feedback, and narrative prompts so momentum never breaks. Whether you are exploring hypotheses or packaging outcomes, the system keeps the right granularity front and center.

  1. 01 Connect

    Link data sources quickly

    Bring in flat files or connect to governed sources. Metadata is inferred, cleansed, and searchable in a single panel.

  2. 02 Collaborate

    Guide the conversation

    Draft prompts, branch ideas, and compare variations. The agent suggests transformations and visuals tailored to your data run.

  3. 03 Validate

    Inspect before you share

    Review SQL, charts, and summary notes side by side. Pin trustworthy answers and log changes for quick audits.

  4. 04 Deliver

    Publish with confidence

    Share dashboards, export snippets, or push events downstream with a persistent audit trail and cost ledger.
