Companion essentials

Answers to the questions teams ask before adopting the assistant.

How does the companion understand our intent?

Prompt blueprints guide the LLM with domain vocabulary, metrics, and safe defaults. FastAPI services inject user context, so suggestions stay relevant and compliant without custom scripting.

Will it respect our data governance rules?

Yes. Access policies live in the API layer, DuckDB connections are scoped per session, and workers operate with scoped secrets. Every action is recorded in an audit trail you can export.

What if the analysis takes longer than expected?

The companion automatically escalates heavy workloads to async workers. You can keep conversing while refreshes, external fetches, or predictive runs complete in the background.

Can we adapt the experience to our rituals?

Absolutely. Tailor prompt libraries, define executive briefing templates, or automate delivery to Slack, email, or dashboards. The companion adapts while preserving best practices.

How do we monitor usage and cost?

Built-in analytics surface prompt spend, worker utilisation, and adoption trends. Alerts can notify stakeholders when thresholds are met.
