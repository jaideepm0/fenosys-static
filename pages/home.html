Prompt

“Diagnose the dip in usage for premium customers and craft steps to stabilise churn before Q3 renewals.”

Companion response

1. Intake & safeguards. Verified role permissions, scanned uploaded CSV and PDF decks, pulled customer health via CRM API, and logged the request with cost and data-lineage tags.

2. Multi-step analysis. Workers profiled churn indicators, ran statistical comparisons, and generated correlation visuals. The LLM streamed interim findings so you could steer in real time.

3. Narrative & assets. Composed a briefing that pairs metrics, annotated trend images, and a rationale outlining assumptions, limitations, and next best experiments.

4. Delivery. Scheduled a refresh job, pushed action items to the control tower, and stored the conversation—ensuring the final response remains auditable and safely reproducible.
